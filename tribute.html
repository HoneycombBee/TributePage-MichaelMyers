Michael Myers

Michael Myers is the killer and antagonist in the Halloween franchise, a series of horror movies under the slasher genre.

Michael Myers:

  • Was born in Haddonfield, Illinois in 1957
  • Murdered his sister in 1963 at six-years-old
  • Has never been known to speak
  • Was admitted into a state hospital after a string of murders on Halloween night, 1978 in his hometown.
